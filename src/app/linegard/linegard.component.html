Assassin's Creed: Lineage!

Assassin's Creed: Lineage is a live-action film series that serves as a prequel to the Assassin's Creed video game franchise. It follows the story of Giovanni Auditore, the father of Ezio Auditore, the main protagonist of Assassin's Creed II. Set in Renaissance Italy, the series delves into Giovanni's role in the secret war between Assassins and Templars, and how his actions set the stage for Ezio's eventual rise as an Assassin. The series is divided into three episodes and provides additional backstory for the game's plot.
