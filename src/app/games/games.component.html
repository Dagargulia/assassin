Discovery Tour - Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Discovery Tour?

The Discovery Tour is an educational game mode by Ubisoft that transforms Assassin’s Creed's historical settings into interactive learning experiences. It allows players to explore history without combat, following guided tours and learning through interactive content.

Did Ubisoft work with historians and experts for the Discovery Tour?

Yes, Ubisoft collaborated with historians, archaeologists, and academic experts to ensure historical accuracy and provide an authentic learning experience in the Discovery Tour.

Do I need to own/play the corresponding Assassin’s Creed games to access the Discovery Tour?

No, the Discovery Tour is available as a standalone title on PC and other select platforms. You do not need the main Assassin's Creed game to access it.

In what languages is the Discovery Tour available?

Discovery Tour supports multiple languages, including English, French, German, Spanish, Italian, Russian, and Japanese. Available languages may vary based on the version and platform.

What are the PC specs to run the Discovery Tour?

The PC specs vary by version. Generally, an Intel Core i5-2400 or AMD FX-4320, 8GB RAM, and an NVIDIA GTX 660 or AMD R9 270 are required for the minimum experience. Recommended specs may vary.
