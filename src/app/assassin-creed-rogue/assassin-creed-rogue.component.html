Game Overview

BECOME THE MOST FEARED ASSASSIN HUNTER IN HISTORY

Experience the Assassin’s Creed universe from the perspective of a Templar. Play as Shay, who, in addition to the deadly skills of a Master Assassin, also possesses never-before-seen skills and weapons.

IMPROVED NAVAL GAMEPLAY

Assassin’s Creed Rogue Remastered builds on the award-winning naval experience from Assassin’s Creed® IV Black Flag®. Cast off in your ship, the Morrigan, and fight your way through the icy seas of the North Atlantic and the narrow waters of America’s river valleys.

BONUS MISSIONS, CUSTOMIZATION PACKS, AND ADDITIONAL BONUS

Two bonus missions from the original edition. Customization packs allowing you to personalize your character. Additional bonus content available via the Ubisoft Club Rewards Program. .
